Information technology coming soon.

Information technology (IT) is the use of any computers, storage, networking and other physical devices, infrastructure and processes to create, process, store, secure and exchange all forms of electronic data. Typically, IT is used in the context of business operations, as opposed to technology used for personal or entertainment purposes. The commercial use of IT encompasses both computer technology and telecommunications.

  • 15 NOV 2021
Fayoum computers at the University of the West of England

Within the framework of the joint project "a cloud-based system for farmers' problems management and decision support for sustainable agriculture in Egypt" between the Faculty of Computers and Information, Fayoum University, and UWE. The Egyptian team visited the University of England, to present what has been achieved in the joint project. The visit began with a tour of UWE University and a meeting with the leaders of the university, then the English team organized a joint workshop between the two teams.
